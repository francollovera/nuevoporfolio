About me

Hello, I'm Franco Llovera, a passionate UX/UI Designer from San Miguel de Tucumán, Argentina, 31 years old. I'm driven by the goal of transforming ideas into visually appealing, functional, and human-centered digital experiences.

Throughout my career, I’ve worked on projects that blend design, development, and strategy. I have experience with WordPress, specializing in website design, layout, and customization to enhance the online presence of brands and professionals. In addition, I have skills in digital marketing, content planning, and social media management, allowing me to connect visual aesthetics with effective communication.

As a Community Manager and content creator, I’ve developed visual strategies for studios, brands, and businesses, optimizing their digital identity and strengthening their connection with the audience. I’m passionate about analyzing user interactions, understanding their needs, and turning those insights into impactful design solutions.

I also have knowledge of tools and technologies such as Figma, Canva, React, Redux, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node, and SQL, which enables me to bridge design and development for consistent, high-quality results.

I’m always ready to take on new challenges and collaborate on unique projects that make a difference. If you’re looking for a creative, analytical, and dedicated professional, don’t hesitate to contact me. Together, we can turn great ideas into digital realities!
