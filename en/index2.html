About me

Hello, I'm Franco Llovera. A passionate UX designer, very dedicated and at the same time a Full Stack web developer specialized in FrontEnd, originally from San Miguel de Tucumán, Argentina, and 29 years old. I have always been fascinated by the world of web design and everything related to the visual aspects of applications. My focus is on creating engaging and functional digital experiences for users.
I have extensive knowledge in figma, content creation and editing, and skills in technologies such as React, Redux, HTML, CSS, Node, SQL and JavaScript. During my education at the UTN of Tucumán, in addition to having the opportunity to learn at Google, my passion for web development led me to embark on an exciting educational journey at the Soy Henry academy and online design courses, where I acquired solid skills and I participated in diverse projects.
I am ready to take on new challenges and collaborate on projects that make a difference. If you are looking for a passionate and dedicated person, do not hesitate to contact me. Together we can turn amazing ideas into reality in the world of web development!
